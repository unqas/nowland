## Strategic Analysis

**At 230 nodes — the Dunbar limit — the Hub achieves asset-sovereignty.** The Hub treasury of **5,966c** (≈€6.0M) is sufficient to purchase commercial real estate, energy infrastructure, and food production. The tax recapture of **€47.7M** over 60 months would fund the construction of a small sovereign village. At this scale, the Hub no longer needs the fiat world — the fiat world needs the Hub.

**Stress Test Comparison**: At the worst idle rate (50% Active (50% Idle)), the Hub treasury drops to **3,513c** (vs. **5,966c** at best case) — a 41% reduction. The floor support still holds.
