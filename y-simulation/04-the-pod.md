## Strategic Analysis

**The Perfect Circle is the minimum scale of true post-fragility.** The [1:1:3:1:1] tier diversity ensures that every Node has a path to mastery, every Elder has a legacy to protect, and the Apprentice's 1.25c generation is covered by the Safety pool. By month 60, the Safety pool of **63.17c** provides a **6-month runway**, and the Hub treasury of **181c** begins purchasing physical reality.

**Stress Test Comparison**: At the worst idle rate (50% Active (50% Idle)), the Hub treasury drops to **106c** (vs. **181c** at best case) — a 42% reduction. Floor failures increase to 1.1, indicating the system needs the MVB protocol.
