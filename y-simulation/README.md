# The Simulation Lab & Thermodynamic Audit

> **"In The Nowland, we do not guess. We simulate."**

This lab provides the high-fidelity proof of the Nowland OS. By modeling the kinetic energy of human labor through different organizational membranes via Monte Carlo simulation, we demonstrate that the transition from a fiat employee to a sovereign Hub node is a mathematical mandate.

---

## Methodology

Every simulation is generated via **100 Monte Carlo iterations** with **±20%** per-node monthly productivity variance. All values shown are the **averaged results** across all runs, ensuring statistical reliability.
