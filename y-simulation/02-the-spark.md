## Strategic Analysis

**The Spark is the highest-risk, highest-velocity phase.** Two founders operating at minimalist 0.9c burn are essentially converting their own biological survival time into structural capital. By month 36, they have accumulated **30.63c** in Hub infrastructure and **€245,009** in tax recapture. The critical question is: when does the accumulated infrastructure justify the transition from the Spark into the first Pod? The data suggests this happens around month 12-24, when the Safety pool can sustain a third member through their onboarding period.

**Stress Test Comparison**: At the worst idle rate (70% Active (30% Idle)), the Hub treasury drops to **25.16c** (vs. **30.63c** at best case) — a 18% reduction. The floor support still holds.
