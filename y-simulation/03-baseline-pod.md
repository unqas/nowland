## Strategic Analysis

**The Baseline Pod proves that you do not need a Perfect Circle to start building.** Five builders at L2 generate enough collective energy to start accumulating real infrastructure. By month 60, the Hub treasury reaches **128c** (≈€127,830). The key limitation is the absence of tier diversity — without an Elder or Architect, the Pod lacks the mentorship gradient that unlocks the Apprentice pipeline and the Helix DNA royalties. This configuration is a stepping stone, not a destination.

**Stress Test Comparison**: At the worst idle rate (50% Active (50% Idle)), the Hub treasury drops to **75.29c** (vs. **128c** at best case) — a 41% reduction. The floor support still holds.
