## Strategic Analysis

**The Subgrid (9 Hubs, 2,070 nodes) achieves regional supply-chain sovereignty.** With **107,474c** in Hub infrastructure (≈€107.5M), the Grid can purchase entire energy grids, agricultural cooperatives, and water infrastructure. The Grid treasury of **36,541c** funds inter-Hub logistics and mutual aid. The tax recapture of **€859.8M** over 120 months represents a complete economic decoupling from legacy supply chains.

**Stress Test Comparison**: At the worst idle rate (70% Active (30% Idle)), the Hub treasury drops to **88,511c** (vs. **107,474c** at best case) — a 18% reduction. The floor support still holds.
