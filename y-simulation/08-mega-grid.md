## Strategic Analysis

**At 100,000 nodes, the Mega Grid out-competes the legacy financial system.** The Hub infrastructure of **5,190,929c** (≈€5.19B) and tax recapture of **€41.53B** over 120 months represent more purchasing power than most nation-states' annual budgets. The Kids Pool of **2,076,372c** means every child born into this ecosystem is born into a pre-funded generational aristocracy of survival. The Helix pool of **3,114,558c** ensures that the system's DNA creators — the architects, engineers, and thinkers — can transition to pure mentorship, sustained indefinitely by compounding royalties.
