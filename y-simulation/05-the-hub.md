## Strategic Analysis

**At 50 nodes, the Hub enters its first formal cooperative membrane.** The structural treasuries grow large enough to fund permanent infrastructure — co-working spaces, shared servers, legal counsel. The Hub treasury of **1,298c** (≈€1.3M) represents real purchasing power. The 6-month Safety runway means that even during a total revenue collapse, no Node hits biological crisis for over half a year. The tax recapture of **€10.4M** over 60 months is the energy that would have been permanently extracted by the fiat state.

**Stress Test Comparison**: At the worst idle rate (50% Active (50% Idle)), the Hub treasury drops to **764c** (vs. **1,298c** at best case) — a 41% reduction. The floor support still holds.
