# Deep Dive: The A-B-C Ledger

We can move the currency from a "measure of energy" to a **"philosophy of existence."**
The progression from **Say** to **Be** to **See** isn't just a math scale; it’s the physical lifecycle of any human achievement. You think it (**Say**), you do the work (**Be**), and then you realize the result (**See**).

## The "Existential" Exchange

Anchoring the hierarchy to your "Pillar" work week, the values scale perfectly with both time and energy:

| Unit | Phonetic / Meaning | Time Value | Energy ($100\text{W}$) | Economic Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 Say (A)** | *Sa* (The Thought) | **10 Seconds** | $1,000\text{ J}$ | A micro-tip or a digital "click." |
| **1 Be (B)** | *Be* (The Action) | **16.7 Minutes** | $100,000\text{ J}$ | **The Core Unit.** The "daily driver." |
| **1 See (C)** | *Cy* (The Result) | **27.8 Hours** | $10,000,000\text{ J}$ | **The Goal.** A "Pillar" or week's output. |

## The "To Be" Transaction (2b)
The social brilliance of this is the verbal exchange. Because "Be" is the middle value (the equivalent of a fiat Dollar or Euro), it becomes the most common word in the economy.

> **Customer:** "How much for the avocado toast and a coffee?"
> **Server:** "That will **To Be**." *(2b)*
> **Customer:** "Fair enough. I’ve been working all morning, I have plenty of **Be** to spend."

It turns every purchase into a reminder that you are trading your "state of being" for the things you want. It's no longer an abstract number; it's a literal slice of your life-force. You are paying exactly **33.4 minutes** of focused "Being" (`2b`) in order to stay alive and continue "to be." It is the perfect linguistic loop.

## The Three Stages of Value

1. **The Say (Intent - 1a):**
   * *Usage:* "I'll give you a few **Says** for that advice."
   * *Feeling:* Light, breezy, essentially frictionless. The cost of an intention.
2. **The Be (Presence - 1b):**
   * *Usage:* "This lunch is **To Be**."
   * *Feeling:* Substantial. You had to sit down and focus for 16.7 minutes to earn it. You are eating the physical fruit of your "Being."
3. **The See (Vision - 1c):**
   * *Usage:* "I finally saved enough to **See** my own server farm."
   * *Feeling:* Heavy and rewarding. It is the culmination of 10,000 individual "Says" or 100 focused "Bes."

## Mathematical Beauty
The scale follows strict metric scaling, making internal calculation effortless:
$$100\text{a} = 1\text{b}$$
$$100\text{b} = 1\text{c}$$

Since **1c** represents nearly an entire 28-hour work week, seeing a Battery (physical asset) priced at **10c** is a visceral deterrent. That isn't just "expensive"—that’s **ten literal weeks** of your 7x4 labor. 

If you view your personal "Energy Bank" app and see a balance of **4c, 20b, 80a**, you intuitively understand your exact physical reality:
* You have **4 weeks** of "Pillar" energy stored (**4c**).
* You have **5.5 hours** of "Focus" energy for today’s spending (**20b**).
* You have some "Change" for micro-transactions or tips (**80a**).

In this system, you aren't spending fiat numbers. You are navigating the world by spending your **A-B-C's**.
