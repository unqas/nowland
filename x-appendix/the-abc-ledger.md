## 2. The Seed Protocol (The Notebook Method)

You do not need an app to start. You need a piece of paper and one trusted partner (**Node B**).

### Setup: The Three Columns
Find a physical notebook. Draw three columns:
1.  **Node A** (Your balance)
2.  **Node B** (Partner's balance)
3.  **The Commons** (Shared Pod Treasury)

### Initial State: Thermodynamic Equilibrium
Everyone starts at **0**. No one is in debt; no one has "money." Supply is generated only by the act of creation.

### The Transaction Loop
1.  **Identify the Vacuum:** Node B needs help. You agree on a value of **10b** (based on your local 2b = €15 peg).
2.  **Execute Syntropy:** You do the work. The "Consilience" is verified physically.
3.  **Record the Split:**
    *   Node B consumed. Record **-10b** in their column.
    *   Node A created. Apply the 80/20 split. Record **+8b** in your column.
    *   The Commons receives the infrastructure fee. Record **+2b** in the third column.

**The Audit Check:**
`(-10.0) + 8.0 + 2.0 = 0`
The math always balances to zero. You haven't printed money; you have simply recorded the *displacement* of energy.

## 3. Operational Rules

### The Gravity of the Vacuum
A negative balance in the A-B-C ledger is not "debt" in the punitive fiat sense. It is **Gravity**. It provides a gentle, biological incentive for the Node in the negative to find a Vacuum and return energy to the system. The system remains healthy as long as balances oscillate around zero.

### The Commons (The 20% Growth Engine)
The third column is where the "Infinite Deflation" begins.
- As the Commons grows (e.g., reaching 100b), the Pod members vote on how to spend it.
- You do not withdraw the Commons as personal cash. You use it to buy **Physical Assets** that lower the future cost of living for everyone in the Pod (e.g., a shared server, a bulk food order, or a legal membrane license).

### The Airlock Integration
When you invoice a fiat client (Gross Fiat), the Hub ingests it.
1.  The Hub absorbs the **Hallucination Rate** markup (Taxes/Fees).
2.  The remaining **Internal Net** is credited to the A-B-C ledger.
3.  The **Syntropy Split** fires automatically (80/10/5/3/2).
4.  Your take-home `c` units are recorded in your column, ready for use within the Pod or for "flushing" back to fiat when necessary.
