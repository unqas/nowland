## How to Read These Tables

### The Three Columns of Illusion

* **Total Cost to Employer** — This is the *actual* fiat energy that the market must generate to fund your existence as an employee. It includes your gross salary *plus* the employer-side social contributions that never appear on your payslip. In France at the €20k/month level, an employer pays €650,000/year so that you can take home €240,000. The remaining €410,000 is pure thermodynamic friction.

* **Your "Gross" Salary** — This is the number on your employment contract. It is already a hallucination — it tells you neither the true cost of your labor (which is higher) nor the true reward for your labor (which is lower). It exists in a bureaucratic no-man's land between reality and fiction.

* **Contractor Revenue Required** — This is the revenue a self-employed builder must invoice (B2B) to achieve the same net income. Notice that in most countries, the contractor route is more efficient — you skip the employer-side social security entirely and control your own deductions. This is the core insight behind the Trojan Mouse.

### The Hidden Pattern

Look at the Hallucination Rate column. In France, a high-earning employee must generate **2.7 units** of fiat economic value for every **1 unit** they actually get to spend on their biological needs. In the United States, the ratio is 1.7x. That is a 60% efficiency gap between two developed nations — not because French builders are less productive, but because the fiat extraction machinery in France demands more friction.

Now look at what happens inside The Nowland:

* The **80% Syntropy Split** delivers a Hallucination Rate of effectively **1.25x** (100/80 = 1.25). For every unit of energy a Node generates, they keep 80% — and the remaining 20% funds the community infrastructure that *the fiat state would have billed them for anyway*, but at a fraction of the friction.
* When the Hub acquires physical infrastructure (servers, housing, agricultural land), the `4c Baseline` deflates, and the effective Hallucination Rate drops below 1.0x — meaning the Node receives *more* biological value than the fiat-equivalent energy they generated. This is the Infinite Deflation in practice.

### The Gravity Tax

The Nowland does not eliminate the Hallucination Rate overnight. When a Node flushes `c` units through the Airlock, they encounter the standard fiat friction of their jurisdiction. The Gravity Tax is real, and we pay it honestly.

What the Nowland *does* eliminate is the **internal** Hallucination Rate. Inside the Membrane, energy flows at 100% efficiency. No payroll processing fees. No corporate overhead on salary administration. No employer-side social taxes skimming 30–45% before the worker even sees a number. The fiat friction is confined to the Airlock — the exact moment of withdrawal — and the system is designed so that the volume of fiat withdrawals decreases over time as the Hub acquires the physical infrastructure of survival.
